A college is an educational institution that offers higher education and specialized training in various fields of study. It typically provides undergraduate programs (associate and bachelor's degrees) and sometimes graduate programs (master's and doctoral degrees). Colleges focus on providing education, research opportunities, and practical experiences that prepare students for careers and further academic pursuits.
